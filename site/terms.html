1Acceptance & Eligibility

By entering NGH, reserving a space, or using our services you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to these Terms & Conditions and any posted house rules. The person who submits a reservation ("Booking Host") is responsible for their entire party's compliance with these terms. Booking Hosts must be at least 18 years old. Anyone under 18 must be supervised as described in Section 11.

2Hours of Operation

"Normal business hours" means the hours NGH is open to the general public, as posted on our Google Maps / Google Business listing and/or on signage within or near Northwood Game Haven at 115 West Spring Street. Hours may change for holidays, private events, or seasonal reasons. Private room reservations may be available outside normal business hours; certain rules below (alcohol, outside food & drink) apply specifically during normal business hours.

3Conduct & Right to Refuse Service

We want NGH to be welcoming for everyone. Guests agree to:

  • Treat staff, other guests, and our space with respect — no harassment, discrimination, threats, intimidation, or hate speech.
  • Refrain from violence, vandalism, theft, or disruptive, unsafe, or unlawful behavior.
  • Follow all reasonable staff instructions and posted rules.
  • Supervise children and keep noise, mess, and activity within reasonable limits.

NGH reserves the right to refuse service, end a session or reservation, or remove any person who violates these terms or whose conduct we determine, in our reasonable discretion, to be unsafe, unlawful, or disruptive — without refund of fees and with potential forfeiture of the deposit.

4Drugs & Alcohol

  • No illegal drugs. The use, possession, sale, or distribution of illegal drugs or controlled substances anywhere on NGH premises is strictly prohibited at all times.
  • No alcohol during normal business hours. The consumption or possession of alcohol during NGH's normal business hours (as posted on Google Maps and/or on signage within or near Northwood Game Haven at 115 West Spring Street) is prohibited, except where NGH itself is licensed to and does serve alcohol under applicable law.
  • Where alcohol is permitted outside of normal business hours (for example, certain private bookings or overnight suites with bar facilities), guests must be of legal drinking age (21+), drink responsibly, and comply with all applicable laws. NGH may refuse service and require removal of alcohol at its discretion.
  • Guests who appear intoxicated or impaired may be refused service or asked to leave for everyone's safety.

5Outside Food & Drink

Because NGH offers in-house concessions and partner-restaurant dining, the following rules apply:

  • No outside food or drink during normal business hours, except as expressly permitted below.
  • Birthday cakes & celebration treats are always welcome — outside birthday cakes and similar celebration treats (e.g., cupcakes for a party) are permitted at any time, including during normal business hours.
  • "Bring Your Own Food & Drink" add-on: During normal business hours, other outside food and non-alcoholic drink is permitted only if the "Bring Your Own Food & Drink" add-on ($40 per reservation) is purchased together with a room reservation.
  • Outside alcohol is never permitted during normal business hours (see Section 4).
  • Guests are responsible for cleaning up after any food or drink and for any allergies or dietary needs of their own party.
🎂 Quick summary: Birthday cakes/treats — always OK. Other outside food & drink during business hours — only with the BYO Food & Drink add-on. Outside food & drink outside business hours (private bookings) — generally permitted, subject to staff guidance.

6Room Reservations & Pricing

  • Bookable play spaces are The Holt, Stash's Den, and The Depths. Rates are $10/hour for the first 4 hours and $5/hour for each hour after, per room.
  • The maximum length of a single booking is 8 hours.
  • Rooms may be reserved nearly any time, including outside normal business hours, subject to availability and any blackout periods NGH sets for events or maintenance.
  • The overnight gaming suites — The Lodge and The Adventurer's Rest — are not booked through the hourly system; they are reserved separately via VRBO or in coordination with NGH staff.
  • Reservations are confirmed only after NGH approves the request and full payment (including the deposit hold) is received per Section 8. NGH may decline or cancel any reservation in its reasonable discretion.
  • Quoted totals do not include applicable taxes required by law.

7Refundable Deposit

A refundable deposit hold is required for every reservation:

  • $40 for one (1) room
  • $80 for two (2) concurrent rooms
  • $100 for all three (3) rooms concurrently
  • +$25 added to the deposit if the Karaoke Equipment add-on is included

The deposit is refunded in full provided that, at the end of the reservation:

  • No games, decorations, consoles, fixtures, or other NGH items are stolen, missing, or destroyed; and
  • The room(s) are left "reasonably cleaned," meaning: any spills or messes were addressed (not necessarily perfectly resolved, but a genuine attempt was made to soak up significant spills and pick up crumbs/messes), trash was disposed of or concentrated in designated disposal areas, and
  • No evident violations of these Terms & Conditions occurred.

If items are damaged, lost, or stolen, or the space is not left reasonably cleaned, or these terms are violated, NGH may withhold all or part of the deposit and, where damages exceed the deposit, charge the Booking Host for the additional reasonable cost of repair, replacement, or cleaning.

8Payment, Cancellation & No-Shows

  • The full amount due — booking fees, paid add-ons, and the refundable deposit hold — must be paid no later than the calendar day before the reservation begins.
  • If payment in full (including the deposit hold) is not received by that day, the reservation will be automatically canceled.
  • Once your reservation is approved, we automatically email you secure online payment links (powered by Stripe) for the booking fee and the refundable deposit — pay online by card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay. You may also pay by cash, card, or check in person, or by card over the phone.
  • Totals do not include applicable taxes required by law.
  • Cancellations, reschedules, and no-shows may be subject to fees as communicated at the time of booking. Approved deposits are released according to Section 7 after a satisfactory post-reservation check.

9Event Registration & Tournaments

  • Some public events (tournaments, league nights, special play days) require registration through our Event Calendar. Each event lists whether registration is free or paid, any minimum number of players needed for the event to run, and any maximum capacity.
  • For events that require a minimum number of registrants to "fire," your spot is confirmed once that minimum is reached. We will email you confirmation; for paid events, that email includes a secure online payment link.
  • Registration fees may be collected at the time of registration or once the event is confirmed, as indicated on each event. When payment is due, you must pay no later than one (1) hour before the event begins, or your spot may be released.
  • If you cancel a paid registration, or if an event is canceled by NGH (including for failing to reach its minimum), any registration fee paid online is refunded automatically to your original payment method.
  • Event prizes, formats, round counts, and rules are described per event and may be adjusted at NGH's reasonable discretion.

10Add-Ons & Equipment Checkout

Add-ons are subject to availability and may be limited or substituted. Included-free add-ons (console/game checkout, board/card game checkout, food pre-orders, karaoke, RPG library, and promotional/supplier support) carry no separate fee but do not waive the deposit or any usage rules. Food pre-orders are available during normal business hours only; ordering ahead is free but the food itself must be paid for and gratuities are not included. Karaoke adds $25 to the deposit. Promotional/supplier support must be coordinated through a certified Game Guru. Paid add-ons (BYO Food & Drink, private Game Guru) are billed as quoted. Checked-out equipment must be returned in the condition received; see Section 10.

The Booking Host is responsible for all members of their party and for any damage to, or loss or theft of, NGH property (games, components, consoles, controllers, furniture, fixtures, décor, AV equipment, and the premises) during their reservation or visit. Such amounts may be deducted from the deposit and, if they exceed it, billed separately.
